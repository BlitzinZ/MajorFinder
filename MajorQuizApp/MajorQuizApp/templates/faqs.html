FAQs About Majors

How do I choose the right major?

Consider your interests, skills, and future career goals. Our quiz helps you match your personality and preferences with majors.

Can I change my major later?

Yes! Most universities allow students to change majors, though some have deadlines or additional requirements.

Are the quiz results final?

No, the quiz is a guide. You should also research careers and talk to academic advisors before deciding.
